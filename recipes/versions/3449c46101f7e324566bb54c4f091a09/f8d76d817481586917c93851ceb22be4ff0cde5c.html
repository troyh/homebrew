http://www.homebrewtalk.com/f12/fullers-esb-clone-229179/ According to http://www.byo.com/component/resource/article/2398-fuller-s-the-pride-of-london, ESB is 5.9% ABV, 35 IBUs, an SRM of 15, an OG of 1.060 and an FG of 1.014. I put the 1.5oz of dry-hop hops in the Blichmann HopRocket which was put inline between the brew kettle and the chiller to see how that affects the hop aroma. I pitched a 1.2L starter because I didn't have enough time to cold-crash and decant. Not ideal. The yeast was also a 2nd generation from a previous batch of Fuller's ESB. Dough-in at 161F.
