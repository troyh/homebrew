Mash temperatures: 154º > 153º > 151.5º > 150.5º

I added the dark grains (Roasted Barley, Chocolate and Special B) at vorlauf rather than mash them for an hour, per Gordon Strong’s suggestion. I sparged for 50 minutes with 169º water and collected 7.2 gallons at 1.052. 

The first wort hops went in while heating to a boil because I wanted to use a hop bag rather than just throw the into the kettle and have to filter them out. The temperature was 176º when they went in.

Pitched yeast and aerated. Forgot yeast nutrient and whirlfloc in the boil so going without this batch.
