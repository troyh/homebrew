Mashed at too low of a temperature. My printout said 148ºF for the mash temp but while mashing, I checked the Brewing Classic Styles book and it was supposed to be 154º. I must’ve been editing the recipe on my iPhone in Brew Pal and forgot to put it back to 154º. D’oh! I guess the yeast is going to bring the FG way down.

Easy brew, but my gravity was way high at the end of the boil, 1.057. So I topped-off the boil kettle with water up to 6.8G, which should have given me a gravity of 1.052, which was my target. I only put 6G into the fermenter.

Pitched the yeast. Didn’t aerate it because the carboy is so full, but maybe I’ll do it later tonight.
